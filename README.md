Power BI Project: Bank CRM & Customer Churn Analysis
🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze customer behavior, churn patterns, and retention risks in a banking environment using interactive Power BI dashboards.

This project helps business stakeholders:

Identify customers likely to churn
Understand customer demographics and behavior
Analyze regional customer distribution
Improve customer retention strategies
Monitor customer engagement and product usage

The dashboard transforms raw banking data into actionable business insights for data-driven decision-making.

🛠️ Technical Toolkit
Visualization Tool: Power BI
Data Processing: Power Query
Data Modeling: Relationships & Star Schema
Language Used: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Source: Excel / CSV Files

📈 Key Insights & Findings
Customer Base: Analyzed 10K+ banking customers across multiple regions.
Churn Rate: Identified an overall churn rate of 20.37%.
Geographical Analysis: France has the highest customer concentration.
Customer Risk Factors: Customers with low credit scores and fewer products showed higher churn probability.
Product Usage: Customers using multiple banking products were more likely to stay retained.
Demographics: The 30–50 age group represented the highest customer segment and churn contribution.
Retention Analysis: Key Influencer visual identified major factors impacting customer exits.

📌 Business Recommendations

✔ Focus on customers with low tenure and low credit scores
✔ Improve engagement strategies for inactive customers
✔ Encourage customers to adopt multiple banking products
✔ Use churn prediction insights for proactive retention campaigns
✔ Monitor high-risk customer segments regularly
